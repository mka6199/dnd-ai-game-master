The Drunken Griffin is the only tavern in the village of Larkspur. Its sign
shows a griffin holding a tankard. The tavern is owned by Borin Stoutfoot,
a retired dwarven ranger who lost his left eye to a manticore. Borin is gruff
but loyal and serves an excellent venison stew. Regular patrons include:

- OLD WICK: a half-elf bard with a flask, knows every rumor in the Vale.
- SISTER ANNETTE: cleric of Lathander, runs the small shrine next door,
  often sits in the corner praying. Will heal injured travelers for a donation.
- CAPTAIN VEX: a half-orc retired sellsword with a glass eye, looking for new
  recruits for "easy work" that is rarely easy.
- THE HOODED FIGURE: never gives a name. Speaks only when spoken to.
  Always pays in gold pieces from a strange black coin pouch.

The tavern serves as a hub for rumors, quests, and chance encounters.
