The Sunken Crypt of Veshara is a three-level dungeon located beneath
the abandoned Thornwood Estate, two miles east of Larkspur village.
It was sealed three hundred years ago when the elven necromancer Veshara
Thornwood was defeated by a coalition of paladins led by Lady Mira's
great-grandmother.

LEVEL 1 - The Forgotten Halls:
- Entrance is a stone trapdoor in the estate's chapel, hidden under a torn rug.
- Long stone corridor with crumbling frescoes depicting Veshara's rise.
- Pressure-plate trap at the midpoint (DC 15 Investigation to spot,
  triggers a poison dart, +5 to hit, 1d4+2 piercing + 1d6 poison).
- The "Hall of Whispers" - a circular room where ghostly voices reveal
  hints about lower levels if a player succeeds DC 13 Wisdom save.
- Guarded by 4 skeletons reanimated by residual necromancy.

LEVEL 2 - The Library of Bones:
- Ancient library with rotting tomes; some still functional spell scrolls.
- Giant spiders have nested here (3-5 of them).
- Multi-stage puzzle: three plinths each requiring a different colored
  gemstone (red, blue, green) found scattered across the floor.

LEVEL 3 - Veshara's Sanctum:
- Final boss chamber. Veshara's preserved phylactery is here.
- Destroying the phylactery completes the main quest.
