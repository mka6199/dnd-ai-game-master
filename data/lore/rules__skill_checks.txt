SKILL CHECKS (D&D 5e)

Common DCs:
- Very Easy: 5
- Easy: 10
- Medium: 15
- Hard: 20
- Very Hard: 25
- Nearly Impossible: 30

Roll: 1d20 + ability modifier + proficiency bonus (if proficient)

Common Skills and Their Ability:
- Athletics (STR): climbing, jumping, swimming, grappling
- Acrobatics (DEX): balance, tumbling, complex maneuvers
- Stealth (DEX): hiding, moving silently
- Sleight of Hand (DEX): pickpocketing, palming objects
- Arcana (INT): magical lore, recognizing spells
- History (INT): historical events, lost civilizations
- Investigation (INT): finding hidden objects, deducing clues
- Nature (INT): natural lore, weather, plants, animals
- Religion (INT): deities, holy rites, religious lore
- Animal Handling (WIS): calming or controlling animals
- Insight (WIS): reading people, detecting lies
- Medicine (WIS): stabilizing dying, diagnosing illness
- Perception (WIS): noticing things in environment
- Survival (WIS): tracking, foraging, navigation
- Deception (CHA): lying convincingly
- Intimidation (CHA): threats, displays of force
- Performance (CHA): entertaining
- Persuasion (CHA): genuine appeals, diplomacy

Passive Perception = 10 + WIS modifier + proficiency (if Perception proficient).
