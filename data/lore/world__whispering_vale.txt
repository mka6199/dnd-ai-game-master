The Whispering Vale is a verdant valley nestled between the Stoneshield Mountains
and the Ember Forest, said to be haunted by the ghost of an elven queen.
The valley's only settlement is the village of Larkspur, ruled by Lady Mira
Thornwood, a half-elf paladin who lost her family to a goblin raid ten years ago.
Larkspur is famous for its silver mines (now mostly tapped out) and the Singing
Stones — naturally resonant crystal formations that hum at dusk.
The Vale is the central hub of the campaign and players often return here between adventures.
